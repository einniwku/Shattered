IAT 337 - Spring 2014

  • Winnie Ku
  • Annie Wang
  • Flora Wu

Using the concept of shattered glass, we recreated a surface that gives off the perception of glass shattering when energy is consumed. The speed and intensity of the shattering gives the users input on the amount of energy consumed; and the sections of the surface represents different areas of the building.
