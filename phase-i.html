Phase I: CRUMPLED

Taking the idea of crumpled paper on the wall, we are using uneven surfaces to represent different areas of a building. The more energy is consumed in that area, the more the surface will move. Using the concept that people will always want to smooth out a crumpled paper, as the consumption lessens, the movement will slow down. And when there is no consumption, the wall will take the form of a smooth paper, as this can never be fully achieved; people will always work towards ‘uncrumpling’ the wall.

The gears will rotate moving the followers which creates an oscillating and up-and-down translation of the surface at different points.
