Configurations

By using our original design as the product platform, we created three product families for different use and placement of the sculpture.

Organic

The first configuration changes the surface of the design while keeping the same gears and movements. The surface here consists of many thin strips of rectangular wood pieces; and the material used will be wood to give an organic feel to the sculpture.

Fish Tank

The second configuration works as a fish tank that will disturb the habitation of the fishes as more energy is used. The surface will move according to the amount of energy consumed of different areas of the building, this will create waves in the fish tank. This discourages the user from over using energy by offering negative feedback.

Ceiling Lights

The last configuration takes the form of lights hanging from the ceiling. As the user consume more energy, the light will be deformed due to the moving and shattering of the surface.
