Phase III: SHATTERED

Concept

Creating a hanging sculpture that gives the perception of shattering glass when moving to discourage users from wasting energy in the building. At zero energy usage, the sculpture will lay flat and smooth allowing for a natural glow from the sun to shine through to the room. When energy usage is detected in the building, the surface will be pulled at different areas (representing different areas of the building) to create breakage on the sculpture making light to shine through at different areas as if the ceiling is shattering.

Using cams and followers to create pulling of surfaces at different areas and distances.
