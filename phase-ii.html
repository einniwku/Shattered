Phase II: CRUMPLED

Keeping to the same concept as phase I, in this phase, we changed the surface of our design to isosceles triangles creating multiple hexagons. This gives the most flexibility for the surface to move and creates interesting movements of the surface when pushed and pulled. The surface will be made from white acrylic to give off the feeling of a white glossy wall when the surface is smooth and undisturbed

The rod will push the surface out at different areas to create the 'crumpleness' of the surface
